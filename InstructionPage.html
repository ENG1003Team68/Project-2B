How does it work??

Welcome to our 'Do It!' fitness app!

I want to get started!

To get started, press the 'Record' button on the home screen.


Selecting an Activity

To record a 'run', you must first select the activity you wish to record on the top-right of the toolbar. At this stage, the options available are walking, running or cycling.

Once you have selected an activity, you are ready to go! Press the play button to start recording!

Finishing a run

When you are finished with your run, they can be saved to keep track of your progress or deleted if you wish to discard the recording.

Viewing past 'runs'

You can view all your previous 'runs' that you have completed in the past in the 'History' section.
